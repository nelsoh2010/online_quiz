Brief profile

Nelson Kudzo Soh is a product of Bishop Herman College, Kpando. He attended Jasikan Teacher Training College and was awarded Teachers’ Cert. ‘A’ from the University of Cape Coast (UCC).

Nelson Kudzo Soh holds Master of Science in IT and B. A. Computer Science Degrees from Sikkim Manipal University, India and University of Ghana, Legon respectively. He is a Tutor and the head of I. T. Unit of Dambai College of Education. N. K. Soh has twelve years (12) teaching experience in the Basic School, Secondary and tertiary levels in Ghana. He has to his credit an article published in an international Peer-Reviewed Journal (IJICTM).

He is the author of a course book entitled A comprehensive Guide to Information and Communication Technology I and II for Schools and Colleges of Education. Also he co-authored the following books; Improving Basic School Pedagogical Skills of College of Education Tutors, Improving the Mentoring Skills of Basic School Teachers for Effective Teaching.

His research interest areas include Integration of ICT into Teacher Education, Mobile App Development and Mentoring.
